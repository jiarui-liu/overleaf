## Key Elements

A method improvement paper proposes a new technique or modification that improves upon existing methods for a known task.

- **Clearly state the baseline method** being improved and its known limitations.
- **Justify each design choice** in the proposed method — readers should understand why each component exists.
- **Provide ablation studies** to show the contribution of each proposed component separately.
- **Compare fairly** against strong, recent baselines under the same experimental conditions.
